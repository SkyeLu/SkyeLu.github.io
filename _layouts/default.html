I am a PhD student at the Image and Visual Representation Lab at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), where I focus on computer vision and computational photography. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sabine Süsstrunk.

Prior to my PhD, I completed my Master's degree at the Deep Vision Lab at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), where I worked with Prof. Jiaya Jia. Before that, I earned my Bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST).
