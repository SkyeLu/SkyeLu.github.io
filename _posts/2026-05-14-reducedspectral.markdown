This is a master’s thesis project that I supervised. We study how spectral dimensionality and representation choice affect illuminant estimation using hyperspectral data. Our results provide practical insights into efficiently exploiting hyperspectral information and reveal conditions under which compact spectral representations outperform conventional RGB-based approaches.
